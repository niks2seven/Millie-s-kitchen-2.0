About Millie’s Kitchen

Founded by Varun Dadhich and Niharika Singh, Millie’s Kitchen brings the warmth of homemade cooking into a restaurant experience. Every dish is crafted with passion, authenticity, and love.

Our goal is simple — to serve fresh, wholesome, and mouthwatering meals that remind you of home, while maintaining the highest restaurant-quality standards.
